## Overview

Groups let you organize users and apply access controls or configurations at scale.

## Common Tasks

- Create a group: Click New Group, name it, and save.
- Add users: Open a group → Members → Add users.
- Remove users: Open a group → Members → Remove.
- Rename or delete: Open a group → Settings → Rename/Delete.

## Tips

- Use groups to scope connectors, actions, or feature access.
- Automated provisioning can manage group membership via your IdP.

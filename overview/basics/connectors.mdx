## What are Connectors?

Connectors bring your organization's data into Onyx. With over 40 connectors for your CRM, shared workspaces, and messaging platforms, Onyx becomes your expert AI teammate and your single source of truth.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Permission-Aware" icon="shield-check">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Reliable and Consistent Results" icon="handshake">
    Onyx will always provide you with the most up-to-date information from your connected data sources.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What's Coming

The Onyx iOS app will enable:

- **Native Mobile Experience**: Optimized interface designed specifically for iOS devices
- **Chat on the Go**: Ask questions and get answers from anywhere using your mobile device
- **Agent Access**: Interact with all your custom AI agents from your phone or tablet
- **Search Capabilities**: Full-text search across your organization's knowledge base
- **Offline Support**: Access recently viewed content even without internet connection
- **Push Notifications**: Get notified about important updates and responses
- **Touch-Friendly Interface**: Intuitive mobile design optimized for touch interactions
- **Secure Authentication**: Seamless integration with your organization's authentication

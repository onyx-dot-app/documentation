## What is Onyx?

Onyx is your team's entry point to Generative AI. Onyx provides a natural language interfaces to all of your team's LLMs and can be connected with your team's knowledge and applications.
Build custom AI Agents, search through documents, research the web, and more.

<Tip>Whether it's a local deployment or Onyx Cloud, your data remains private and is never used to train models.</Tip>

## Why Onyx?

Why choose Onyx over ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, Google Gemini, or Glean?

1. **Reliable Responses from Internal Knowledge:**
Onyx builds a knowledge index using LLM-native techniques. Powered with contextual retrieval, advanced RAG, hybrid search, and AI-generated knowledge graphs, Onyx ensures the most relevant results and the least hallucinations.

2. **Open Source:**
Onyx is made for security, data privacy, and ease of self-hosting. It's easy and free to get started. For teams investing in a long-term GenAI strategy, Onyx can be easily extended or customized to your team's future needs.

3. **Highly Configurable:**
Onyx is designed to be flexible, so you can optimize the experience for your team. Plug and play any LLM model, connect only the data you need, and enable the features you want your team to use.

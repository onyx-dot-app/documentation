## General Security

### How does Onyx protect my data?
Onyx implements multiple layers of security including encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access controls, comprehensive audit logging, and secure data processing pipelines.

### What encryption standards does Onyx use?
Onyx uses AES-256-GCM for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. All encryption keys are managed through secure key management systems.

### How does Onyx handle authentication?
Onyx supports multiple authentication methods including SAML 2.0, OIDC, OAuth 2.0, and LDAP/Active Directory integration for enterprise deployments.

## Data Privacy

### Where is my data stored?
Data storage location depends on your deployment choice. Self-hosted deployments keep all data on your infrastructure, while cloud deployments use secure cloud storage in your chosen region.

### Does Onyx access my data?
Onyx personnel do not have access to your data. All access is logged and audited, and data processing occurs in isolated environments.

### How long is my data retained?
Data retention policies are configurable. You can set custom retention periods and implement automatic data deletion policies.

## Compliance

### What compliance standards does Onyx meet?
Onyx is designed to meet various compliance standards including SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA, and others depending on deployment configuration.

### Can Onyx support my specific compliance requirements?
Yes, Onyx can be configured to meet specific compliance requirements. Contact our team to discuss your specific needs.

## Security Incidents

### How does Onyx handle security incidents?
Onyx has comprehensive incident response procedures including immediate threat detection, automated response, and customer notification protocols.

### What is Onyx's security incident notification process?
Security incidents are communicated to customers according to severity and impact, with detailed incident reports and remediation steps provided.

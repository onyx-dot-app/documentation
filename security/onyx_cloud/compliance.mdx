# Compliance

This page details Onyx Cloud's compliance certifications and support for various regulatory requirements.

## Compliance Certifications

### Security Standards
- **SOC 2 Type II**: Annual Service Organization Control 2 Type II audits
- **ISO 27001**: Information Security Management System certification
- **ISO 27017**: Cloud-specific information security controls
- **ISO 27018**: Protection of personally identifiable information in public clouds

### Privacy Standards
- **GDPR**: Full compliance with General Data Protection Regulation
- **CCPA**: California Consumer Privacy Act compliance
- **LGPD**: Brazilian General Data Protection Law compliance
- **Other Privacy Laws**: Support for various international privacy regulations

## Industry-Specific Compliance

### Healthcare
- **HIPAA**: Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act compliance
- **HITECH**: Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health Act
- **BAAs**: Business Associate Agreements for healthcare organizations
- **PHI Protection**: Protected Health Information safeguards

### Financial Services
- **PCI DSS**: Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard
- **SOX**: Sarbanes-Oxley Act compliance
- **GLBA**: Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act compliance
- **Financial Regulations**: Support for various financial industry regulations

### Government
- **FedRAMP**: Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program
- **FISMA**: Federal Information Security Management Act
- **State and Local**: Support for state and local government requirements
- **International**: Support for international government requirements
